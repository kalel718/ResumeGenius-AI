## What It Does
ResumeGenius AI analyzes your resume against any job posting and provides:
- ATS compatibility score (0-100%)
- Matched vs missing keywords
- Resume structure analysis
- Actionable improvement recommendations

## The Problem
75% of resumes are rejected by Applicant Tracking Systems before humans see them.
Most job seekers don't know WHY their resume was rejected.

## The Solution
AI-powered analysis that shows exactly what ATS systems see and how to optimize.

## Technology
- **Sentence Transformers** - Semantic text similarity
- **scikit-learn** - Cosine similarity calculations
- **Gradio** - Web interface
- **all-MiniLM-L6-v2** - 384-dimension embedding model
